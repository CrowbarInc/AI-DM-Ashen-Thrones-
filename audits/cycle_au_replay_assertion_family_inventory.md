# Cycle AU1 Replay Assertion Family Inventory

## Purpose

This inventory maps the major assertion families currently concentrated in
`tests/test_golden_replay.py` to explicit owner buckets and movement decisions
for Cycle AU golden replay ownership compression.

Scope is mapping only. This artifact does not move tests, change replay
behavior, change expected diagnostics, or alter golden replay coverage.

Source: `cycle_au_golden_replay_ownership_compression_recon.md`.

## Decision Vocabulary

- `stays in golden replay`: remains in `tests/test_golden_replay.py` as hard replay/orchestration coverage.
- `moves to projection owner`: future candidate for replay projection contract coverage.
- `moves to diagnostics/report owner`: future candidate for dashboard/report/artifact contract coverage.
- `moves to drift taxonomy owner`: future candidate for rerun drift, owner drift, trend, hotspot, or risk coverage.
- `moves to fallback/final-emission owner`: future candidate for fallback, final-emission, lineage, or owner-bucket proof modules.
- `helper extraction candidate`: assertion shape may remain golden-facing but should be expressed through shared helpers.
